Classroom Expectations & Procedures

Classroom Expectations

  • Respect Each Other: Treat others how you would treat yourself, remember we are all here together to build our knowlege as a class.
  • Stay Curious: Actively participate in discussions, ask questions, and explore libraries online to learn things that you want to learn. Computer science is a very self guided subject.
  • Follow Digital Citizenship Guidelines: Use technology responsibly and be sure to practice our online safety procedures.

Classroom Procedures

  • Starting Class: Enter quietly, take your seat, and review the daily objective on the board. Begin the warm-up activity within the first five minutes of class.
  • Submitting Assignments: Submit all assignments via the Canvas Assignment page by the due date. You are responsible for testing your code before submitting.
  • Online Safety: Be sure to use two factor authentication on all accounts, only use school emails for Git.
