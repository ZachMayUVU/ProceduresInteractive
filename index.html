Classroom Expectations & Procedures

Classroom Expectations

  • Respect Everyone and Everything: Treat classmates, the teacher, and equipment with kindness and care to foster a positive learning environment.
  • Stay Curious and Engaged: Actively participate in discussions, ask questions, and explore new ideas to build your Computer Science skills.
  • Follow Digital Citizenship Guidelines: Use technology responsibly, stay on task, and follow ethical coding and research practices.

Classroom Procedures

  • Starting Class: Enter quietly, take your seat, and review the daily objective on the board. Begin the warm-up activity within the first five minutes of class.
  • Submitting Assignments: Submit all assignments digitally via the classroom portal by the due date. Confirm submission by checking the “Submitted” status.
  • Handling Equipment: Before using computers, sign out a device using the classroom log. Log out, shut down, and return it to its station before leaving.
